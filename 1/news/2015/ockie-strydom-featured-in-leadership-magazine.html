    • Ockie Strydom featured in Leadership magazine

    15 September 2015

    Recently, one of the founders of the Elegant Group, Ockie Strydom, chatted to Cape Media for a piece in the September 2015 edition of Leadership magazine.

    As an award-winning flagship publication of Cape Media, Leadership magazine is one of the oldest and most respected voices for South Africa's business and political leaders, providing insightful and intelligent conversation.

    The article touched on all things Elegant and its unique approach to business as more than just a provider of fuel; it stands out as a proudly South African organisation that places hard work and good leadership above all else.
