    Supplying fuel when you need it, where you need it

    Grace Refill Oil & Gass Group was opened 1 years ago when we recognised the need for a reliable fuel supplier that could cater to smaller users of petroleum. Today, fuel remains our core business and we’re one of the largest independent fuel providers in Ujemen Edo, Nigeria.

    We pride ourselves on being Nigeria owned, reliable, hands-on and committed to quality service – 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

    We’re proudly Nigeria

    Grace Refill Oil & Gass is a proudly Nigeria fuel wholesaler, distributor and service provider. We started locally, are locally owned, and strongly believe in growing the local economy, uplifting local individuals and communities while we do so.

    We’re reliable

    Grace Refill Oil & Gass offers clients a steady supply stream with 24-hour delivery schedules and, equally importantly, round-the-clock communication. We boast extensive network coverage and can provide a reliable supply stream of all our products, thanks to our state-of-the-art equipment and our excellent relationships with our suppliers.

    Grace Refill Oil & Gass can leverage our relationships with the oil majors to ensure that we always have product available, which gives us a considerable competitive advantage. Thanks to our high-level relationships with the big oil companies, nurtured over time, we can collect fuel at any depot or refinery.

    We’re hands-on

    Our hands-on directors have more than 10 years’ combined experience in the field and take ownership of the day-to-day running of the company. This ensures that there are always extremely knowledgeable people on hand to deal with customers’ needs.

    We’re committed to quality, client-focused service

    Nobody gets their fuel as punctually (and in many cases as early) as Grace Refill Oil & Gass clients. We provide all our clients – no matter how big or small they are – with impeccable personal service.

    Our continuous growth over the past years, as well as our list of long-standing clients, are testament to the fact that we don’t see ourselves as merely a business with clients paying for and receiving fuel – we build relationships with clients, helping them to make the most of their businesses, which in turn helps to grow the wealth of our country.
