Why should we be your wedding photographers:

We truly understand that your wedding day will take months or even years of planning, and the vows you take will stay with you for the rest of your life.

By choosing us as your wedding photographers, we will guide you through the exciting journey of making images, and brings your wedding day flooding back, every time you open your album.

Click through our galleries, and you will soon realise why couples from, not only Pretoria & Gauteng, but throughout Southern Africa, insist on partnering with us for  wedding memories. Images that capture the essence of love, romance and commitment.

Whether we’re attending a celebration in our hometown Pretoria, or travelling to the most romantic wedding destination around the globe, we are by your side. Capturing your memories beautifully is what makes us happy.

Your wedding photos are special moments, preserved forever, ensuring that your loving glance or playful smile is there for you to share with friends and family for years to come.

 

Not only does Warren hold a world-renowned accolade from Fujfilm as an international  ambassador & X-Photographer,  Fujifilm’s highest recognition awarded to a select group of photographers, Warren strives to use that skill and experience to guide you on the exciting journey of wedding photography .

By partnering with us as your wedding photographers, you will have your unique story told through images.  A collection of beautiful photographs that will only get more valuable to you in years to come.
